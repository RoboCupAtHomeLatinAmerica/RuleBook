\section{Competition}
The competition consists of Two \emph{Sub-leagues}.
Each sub-league comprises four \iterm{Tasks} inside the arena and one \iterm{Task} outside the arena.
In both sub-leagues the competition ends with the \FINAL{} where the two highest ranked teams of each sub-league compete to win.
